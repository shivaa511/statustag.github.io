Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy was last changed on 24-06-2020.

The Company, Status TAG, is accessible from https://www.statustag.com. This page intends to brief you on our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal data we collect from users of the site.

The company handles your Personal Information only for the use and enhancement of the site. By using the site, you agree to the acquisition and use of information following this policy.

If you have questions or require additional information concerning our Privacy Policy, connect to us. This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for our website visitors to Status TAG. This policy does not apply to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website.



Consent
By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Information Collection and Use

To use our site, we may ask you to provide us a few personally identifiable data that can contact or identify you. Personally, identifiable information may incorporate but is not narrowed to your name.

Further, if you contact us directly, we may receive more information about you, like your full name, email address, phone number, the content of the message or attachments that you send us, or any data you shared with us.

For registering yourself with an Account at our site, we may ask you for your contact information, which includes items such as name, company name, address, email address, and telephone number.


Geo-Location Information

We may request access or permission to and track location-based information from your mobile device, either continuously or while you are using the Application, to provide location-based services. If you wish to change our access or permissions, you may do so in your device’s settings.


Push Notifications

We may request to send you to push notifications regarding your account or the Application. If you wish to opt-out from receiving these types of communications, you may turn them off in your device’s settings.


Third-Party Data

Information from third parties, such as personal information or network friends: For the cases when you connect your account to the third party and grant the application permission to access the data.

  • Send you emails
  • Find and prevent fraud

Therefore, we cannot guarantee complete security if you provide personal information.


Log Files

Status TAG follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services’ analytics. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. The log data do not link with any personally identifiable information. The purpose of the data is to analyze trends, to administer the site, to track users’ movement on the website, and to gather demographic information. We create our Privacy Policy with the help of the Privacy Policy Generator and the Privacy Policy Template.


CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)

Under the CCPA, among other rights, users have the right to request that a business that collects a consumer’s data disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal data that a company has collected about consumers. Request that a business deletes any personal data about the consumer that a company has collected. Request that a business that sells a consumer’s data, not sell the consumer’s data. If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.


GDPR Data Protection Rights

We want to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. It entitles every user to the right to access–You may request copies of your data. We may charge you a small fee for this service. The right to rectification–You may request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also may request that we complete the information you think is incomplete. The right to erasure–You may request that we erase your data under certain conditions The right to restrict processing–You may request that we restrict the processing of your data under certain conditions The right to object to processing–You may object to our processing of your data under certain conditions. The right to data portability-You may request that we transfer the data we have collected to another organization or directly to you under certain conditions. If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.


Children’s Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, take part in, and monitor and guide their online activity. Status TAG does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately, and we will do our best to remove such information from our records promptly.
