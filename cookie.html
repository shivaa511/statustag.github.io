What are Cookies?

Cookies are a small chunk of data stored in text files and saved on your computer or any other devices when a browser loads a website.

Cookies are primarily adopted to memorize you and your selected preferences, either for a single visit (through a “session cookie”) or for repeated visits (using a “persistent cookie”).

Session cookies are transitory cookies which are used during the session of your visit to the website, and they lapse once you close the web browser.

Persistent cookies are to recall your preferences inside our website and persist on your desktop or mobile device even after you choose to close your browser or to restart your computer. They establish a consistent and active involvement for you during your visit to our website or while using our Services.

Cookies can either be collected by the website (“first-party cookies”), or by any third parties who serve for the content or provide advertising or analytics assistance for the website (“third party cookies”). These third parties can identify you whenever you tour our website and also when you visit certain other websites.



What type of Cookies do we use?
Necessary Cookies

Necessary Cookies let us offer you the best possible experience while evaluating and maneuver through our website and benefiting its features. For instance, these cookies allow us to recognize the account you have created and help to log into that account to access the content.

Functionality Cookies

Functionality cookies allow websites to remember the user’s site preferences and choices they make on the site, including username, region, and language.
